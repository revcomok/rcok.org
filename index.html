Are you ready to fight?

The Trump regime is removing every safeguard that the capitalist class has used to prop up United States capitalism since 2008. In just a few short months, the economy has been plunged into disarray and workers are already taking to the streets in the largest mass movement since the Black Lives Matter movement! The workers are already on the move in Oklahoma and internationally. To channel all of that energy into victory, we need a mass communist party now!

Neither major US party represents the working class of the United States of America. The only force capable of transforming society is the organized and mobilized working class itself. The Revolutionary Communist International has made outstanding progress in the recent period, growing by leaps and bounds. The Revolutionary Communists of America are growing explosively in cities across the US, with cells in every major metropolitan area in the country.

The building of a revolutionary organization—in Oklahoma and around the world—is the immediate need; not in the remote future, but now. We are determined to carry out this historic task. But in order to achieve it, we need you!

Build the Party of the Revolutionary Communists of Oklahoma

Neither major US party is coming to save us. In order to fight and win, we need to build a revolutionary communist party! That is why we have launched the Revolutionary Communists of Oklahoma (RCOK), affiliated with (but not endorsed by) the Revolutionary Communists of America, part of the worldwide Revolutionary Communist International, which is active in over 70 countries around the world.

There are thousands of revolutionary communists in Oklahoma ready to organize and fight for revolution. If you're a socialist or communist and you're committed to the overthrow of capitalism in our lifetime, then the Revolutionary Communists of Oklahoma is your party. Now is the time for action!
